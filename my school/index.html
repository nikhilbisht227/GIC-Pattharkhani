Welcome to School Website of GIC Pattharkhani


School Management System is a complete school management software designed to automate a school's diverse functions, from class management to exams and events scheduling. This software provides an interactive platform for parents, teachers, and students. It streamlines administrative tasks, making them fast, easy, efficient, and accurate.


The system maintains various records related to students, including general details (such as student names, addresses, performance, and attendance) and specific department-related information.
